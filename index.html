NBA Draft Pick vs Salary:

Plot:



This Visualization looks to understand the relationship between what draft pick a player is and their salary. By uisng the radio button you can switch the graph to see the relationship between draft pick rating in NBA 2k20. By looking at the Visualization you can see that their are more high picks with high salaries and as the draft pick gets lower, there are less salaries in the top half. One more interesting thing that can be see are the rookie contracts which form a sloping line in the bottom quarter of the graph. The relationship between draft pick and rating is negative, meaning as draft pick increases, the rating will most likely decrease. The main take away is that high draft picks typically have a higher rating and higher salary.
I choose the color purple because it contrasts nicely with the white background. I choose points as my marks and controlled them with horizontal and verttical position. This was the best way to represent the 429 players for the 2 variables I was looking into.
